Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and communication.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard website project typically takes 4-8 weeks from discovery to launch. Mobile apps may take 8-16 weeks depending on features. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation phase.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages including monthly maintenance, security updates, performance monitoring, and content updates. Our support plans are tailored to your specific needs and budget. We provide 30 days of free support after project launch.

What industries do you primarily work with?

We have experience across multiple industries including healthcare, finance, education, e-commerce, SaaS, and professional services. Our team adapts to the specific needs and regulations of each industry to deliver compliant and effective solutions.

How do you handle project communication?

We use a combination of communication tools including scheduled meetings (in-person or video), project management software (like Asana or Trello), email updates, and phone calls. Each project has a dedicated project manager who serves as your primary point of contact.

What is your payment structure?

We typically use a phased payment structure: 30% upfront to begin the project, 40% upon completion of design/development milestones, and 30% upon project completion and before launch. For larger projects, we can discuss custom payment plans.
